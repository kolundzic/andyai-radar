# Top AI Repos 2026 — AndyAI Radar Report

## Subtitle

From open-source hype list to governed AI stack map.

## Executive Summary

AI development in 2026 is no longer centered around one model or one chatbot.

The modern AI stack is becoming layered: models, local runtimes, user interfaces, RAG/context engines, agent logic, workflow automation, app builders, developer cockpits and action agents.

This report maps the first AndyAI Radar signal — Top AI Repos 2026 — into a practical evaluation matrix for builders, founders, agencies and IT teams.

## Repos Covered

- n8n
- Ollama
- Open WebUI
- RAGFlow
- LangGraph / LangChain
- Dify
- Langflow
- Gemini CLI
- DeepSeek-V3
- OpenClaw-style agents

## Governance Rule

No AI tool becomes part of the AndyAI stack because it is popular. It enters the roadmap only after signal review, stack classification, business value test, risk and permission review, evidence check and human approval.
