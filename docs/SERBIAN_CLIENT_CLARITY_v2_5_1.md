# v2.5.1 — Serbian Client Clarity + Offer Simplification

## Purpose

This release reduces public-facing complexity and shifts AndyAI Radar toward clear Serbian client communication.

## Public message

AI bez magle. Jasan pregled. Pametan izbor. Siguran prvi korak.

## External positioning

AndyAI Radar helps companies choose the right AI tools, avoid expensive mistakes, and create the first safe AI system for work.

## Three primary offers

1. AI Pregled za firmu
2. AI Asistent za vaše dokumente
3. AI Automatizacija posla

## Rule

Internal architecture may remain deep:
- stack map
- governance
- risk register
- permission matrix
- registry

But public sales language must stay simple:
- what problem we solve
- what the client gets
- what the first step is
- where safety matters
